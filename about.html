Why I Started Cooking

After spending long hours sitting at a desk on a laptop, I realized the importance of taking breaks and engaging in activities that not only relax the mind but also provide physical relief. Cooking became my go-to hobby as it allowed me to stand up, move around, and rest my forearms after a day of continuous typing.

About This Cooking Website

This cooking website was created with a simple philosophy: no unnecessary tracking cookies and no life stories before the recipes. I believe that cooking should be enjoyable and accessible to everyone without having to sift through endless personal anecdotes. Here, you'll find a collection of delicious recipes without any distractions.
