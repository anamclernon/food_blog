Banana raspberry pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1.25 cups milk
  • 1 banana
  • Some frozen or fresh raspberries

Instructions

  • Step 1: Mix dry ingredients, then wet, then bananas and raspberries.
  • Step 2: Make little pancakes.
