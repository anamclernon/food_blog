Banana peanut butter cake

I was missing greek yoghurt, butter, and baking soda so I improvised. Also the batter was very dry so I went wild and added milk too. This cake was delish.

Ingredients

  • 2.5 cups flour
  • 0.75 cup sugar
  • 3.5 teaspoons baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 0.5 cup olive oil (or can try 1 cup butter)
  • 0.5 cup magerquark (or try greek yoghurt)
  • 0.5 cup milk
  • 2 bananas
  • 3 tablespoons of peanut butter

Instructions

  • Step 1: Heat oven to 180 C.
  • Step 2: Mix all the dry ingredients and then mix in all the wet ingredients. Finally add banana and peanut butter.
  • Step 3: Put in the oven for approx. 45 minutes.
