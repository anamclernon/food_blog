Broccoli cottage cheese pie

The pastry was not great. But the filling was nice. Perhaps my filling was too liquid and so it was difficult to hold together.

Ingredients

  • 200 g flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 0.25 cup olive oil
  • Some broccoli
  • Some cottage cheese
  • Mozarella
  • Some parmesan
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  • Step 1: Heat oven to 190 C.
  • Step 2: Put flour in bowl and make a well in center. comine oil with 125 ml of warm water and salt. Knead the dough for about 5-6 minutes. Press dough into a disk, wrap with plastic and leave dough in the fridge for 30 minutes.
  • Step 3: Cook garlic and broccoli. Mix eggs, broccoli, all the cheeses in a bowl.
  • Step 4: Form pastry into two circles, one smaller than the other. Put bigger one at bottom of a baking tray. Add mixture and put smaller circle of pastry on top. Then fold over the edges.
